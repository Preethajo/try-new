About Us

Try new is a sister concern of Go Smart Institution which had marked their reputation for quality and integrity for more than 12 years in the education sector.We had diversified into food service with the same level of quality and integrity to treat our customers as family guests.
Try New was founded in 2020 with one big dream. we started with Idli/Dosa Batter with the group of passionate womens, we worked with enthusiastic and with love, to ensure people would get healthy and hygienic food. Our products were also widely appreciated amoung our clients.We assure every food delivered should be good and wholesome, we maintain the highest level of quality of hygiene as the same are prepared with love and affection with motherly assurance and standard.
